SYNTH COLOR FILTER

The synth color filter is an experiment in subtractive synthesis built at the 7/9 NYC Monthly Music Hackathon. Press play and mouse over the color wheel to get started. When you select a color a filter is applied to the soundwave that only lets the frequencies used to generate the selected color soon. The filter is most intense towards the edge of the circle, and least intense at the very center.
